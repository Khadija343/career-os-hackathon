# Career OS - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Version

1.0

---

# Product Vision

Career OS is an AI-powered career operating system that helps students and early professionals become internship and job ready through intelligent analysis, personalized guidance, and continuous career tracking.

Rather than offering generic advice, Career OS understands a user's profile, goals, projects, resume, and GitHub portfolio to provide actionable recommendations.

---

# Problem Statement

Students face several challenges:

- Unsure what to learn next.
- Weak resumes.
- Poor GitHub portfolios.
- Lack of personalized career guidance.
- No centralized platform for career development.
- Difficulty preparing for internships and technical interviews.

---

# Product Goal

Build an intelligent assistant that guides users from learning to employment.

---

# Target Audience

- University Students

- Self-taught Developers
